Keep Your Children Safe

SafeChild QR provides secure, offline QR codes for your children. Print, attach, and have peace of mind knowing help is just a scan away.

How It Works

1. Create Account

Sign up securely as a parent. Your data is encrypted and protected.

2. Add Your Child

Enter your child's information and emergency contact details.

3. Generate QR

Create an offline QR code with all emergency information embedded.

4. Print & Attach

Print the QR code and attach it to your child's clothing or backpack.

100% Offline & Private

All data is embedded in the QR code itself. No internet required to scan. No personal information stored online. Complete privacy and security.
